Image Nation Media is an independent film studio and creative collective based in Los Angeles. We produce features, shorts, music videos, and experimental art for artists who refuse to be categorized and have a bigger story to tell.  We are not interested in content. We are not interested in trends. We are interested in raising awareness and invoking a deeper way of thinking.
